# Hero Video

Drop your video file here as:

  client/public/video/hero.mp4     ← primary (H.264, recommended)
  client/public/video/hero.webm    ← optional fallback (VP9)

## Tips

- Aim for under 8 MB for fast loading
- Landscape orientation, 1920×1080 or 1280×720
- No audio needed (the video is muted by default)
- Good sources: Pexels, Mixkit, Coverr (free), or your own footage

## Suggested search terms

- "person sunflower field" — pexels.com/search/videos/sunflower
- "nature walk golden hour"
- "mindfulness outdoor"

The video loops seamlessly and is covered by a dark gradient overlay,
so slightly over-exposed or bright footage works well.

If no video file is present, the site falls back to an Unsplash
sunflower field photo automatically.
